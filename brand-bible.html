House of Ross
Divisional Charter & Brand Bible

A manuscript of lineage, structure, and creative governance for the House of Ross.

Introduction

The House of Ross is a lineage‑driven creative dynasty built on care, ritual, and mythology. This Divisional Charter & Brand Bible defines the structure, purpose, and governance of each Division within the House, and is preserved within the Library & Research Division as an official reference manuscript.

The House of Ross

The House of Ross is the overarching realm that unifies all creative, commercial, and mythic activity. All Divisions operate under its banner and uphold its values of lineage, emotional intelligence, and long‑term stewardship.

Ross Creative Division

The imaginative engine of the House of Ross.

Charter

The Ross Creative Division shapes concepts, narratives, visual languages, and mythic frameworks that define the identity of the House. It is responsible for world‑building, emotional architecture, and the creation of original intellectual property across all mediums.

Mandate

  • Develop creative concepts and story systems.
  • Establish and protect creative and narrative canon.
  • Direct cross‑medium artistic and visual output.
  • Maintain the emotional and mythic tone of the House.
