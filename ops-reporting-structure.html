Overview

The Reporting Structure defines who answers to whom, how decisions move, and how accountability is held.

It connects the Studio Wing, the Consultancy Wing, and the Guardian Quadrant into one coherent chain of responsibility.

Top Level

House of Ross Director

Ultimate creative and operational authority. Holds the lineage, vision, and final sign‑off.

Studio Lead

Oversees all creative departments and universes.

Consultancy Lead

Oversees all client‑facing, governance, and professional operations.

Studio Wing Reporting

How creative departments report upwards.

Creative Direction

  • Reports to: Studio Lead
  • Collaborates with: Mythic Architecture, Animation

Mythic Architecture

  • Reports to: Studio Lead
  • Collaborates with: Creative Direction, Research & Lore

Animation Division

  • Reports to: Studio Lead
  • Collaborates with: Creative Direction, Music & Sound

Textile & Fashion Lab

  • Reports to: Studio Lead
  • Collaborates with: Emotional Architecture, Archive

Music & Sound

  • Reports to: Studio Lead
  • Collaborates with: Animation, Emotional Architecture

Emotional Architecture

  • Reports to: Studio Lead
  • Collaborates with: All creative departments

Consultancy Wing Reporting

How corporate departments report upwards.

Client Experience

  • Reports to: Consultancy Lead
  • Collaborates with: Back Office, Governance & Legal

Governance & Legal

  • Reports to: Consultancy Lead
  • Collaborates with: All departments for compliance

Back Office

  • Reports to: Consultancy Lead
  • Supports: Entire House operationally

Ross Consultancy Division

  • Reports to: Consultancy Lead
  • Interfaces with: Clients, Studio Lead, Director

Guardian Quadrant Reporting

The AI governance layer supports, advises, and documents — it does not replace human authority.

Everator

Advises: Director, Studio Lead, Consultancy Lead on narrative and mythic coherence.

Strategist AI

Advises: Governance & Legal, Back Office, Director on operations and compliance.

Archivist AI

Supports: All departments with documentation, precedent, and archive.

Muse AI

Supports: Creative departments with ideation and emotional architecture.
