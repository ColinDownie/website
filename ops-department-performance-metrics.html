Overview

Performance Metrics define how each department’s effectiveness, alignment, and contribution to the House of Ross are evaluated.

These metrics ensure clarity, accountability, emotional intelligence, and mythic coherence across the dynasty.

Metric Categories

The core dimensions of performance.

1. Operational Metrics

  • Task completion rate
  • Workflow efficiency
  • Resource utilisation
  • Turnaround time

2. Creative Metrics

  • Innovation quality
  • Concept resonance
  • Cross‑department creative impact
  • Emotional architecture alignment

3. Strategic Metrics

  • Long‑term alignment
  • Contribution to House goals
  • Structural impact
  • Future‑proofing

4. Cultural Metrics

  • Wellbeing contribution
  • Emotional intelligence
  • Collaboration quality
  • House culture alignment

5. Canon Metrics

  • Mythic coherence
  • Lineage protection
  • Narrative alignment
  • Everator approval

Review Cycle

How performance is evaluated over time.

Quarterly Review

Operational and creative performance.

Annual Review

Strategic alignment and structural impact.

Canon Review

Everator evaluates mythic and lineage coherence.

AI Quadrant Audit

Strategist, Archivist, and Muse provide analysis.

Scoring System

How performance is quantified.

1. Baseline

Minimum expected performance for the department.

2. Standard

Healthy, consistent performance aligned with House expectations.

3. Elevated

Performance that strengthens the House and advances lineage.

4. Mythic

Exceptional performance that becomes part of House canon.
