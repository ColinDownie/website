Preface

This Charter & Brand Bible establishes the formal structure, purpose, and operational mandates of the Divisions within the House of Ross. It is preserved within the Library & Research Division as a reference manuscript for governance, lineage continuity, and organisational clarity.

The House of Ross

The House of Ross is a lineage‑driven creative dynasty built on care, ritual, and mythology. It unifies all creative, commercial, and mythic activity under a single banner, and upholds values of emotional intelligence, heritage, and long‑term stewardship.
