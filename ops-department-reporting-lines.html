Overview

Reporting lines define how authority, responsibility, and communication flow through the House of Ross.

This structure ensures clarity, accountability, and mythic coherence across all departments.

Top-Level Structure

The highest tier of authority.

Director

Ultimate authority; oversees all departments and approves major decisions.

Governance & Legal

Reports to Director; ensures compliance, risk management, and structural integrity.

Guardian Quadrant

Advisory layer; supports Director with strategy, canon, memory, and emotional intelligence.

Mid-Level Structure

The operational leadership tier.

Studio Lead

Reports to Director; oversees all creative and production departments.

Consultancy Lead

Reports to Director; oversees client-facing and professional service departments.

Back Office Lead

Reports to Governance & Legal; manages admin, finance support, and scheduling.

Department-Level Structure

How individual departments connect upward.

Creative Departments

Report to Studio Lead; collaborate with Muse AI and Everator.

Operational Departments

Report to Back Office Lead; collaborate with Strategist AI.

Consultancy Departments

Report to Consultancy Lead; collaborate with Governance & Legal.

AI Quadrant Departments

Report directly to Director; support all other departments.

Communication Flow

How information moves through the House.

Upward Flow

Departments → Leads → Director → Archive

Downward Flow

Director → Leads → Departments

Lateral Flow

Departments collaborate across wings with AI Quadrant support.
