Overview

The Clan System is the social and emotional architecture of the House of Ross — two ancient factions, Green and Orange, whose energies shape the world Z inherits.

Their rivalry is ritual, not war. Their differences are creative, not destructive. Their fusion is destiny.

The Clans

The three factions of the House.

Green Clan

Energy: Calm, strategic, grounded

Element: Earth

Symbol: The Verdant Crest

Beverage: Green Elixir (herbal, cooling, clarifying)

Role: The planners, the thinkers, the stabilisers.

Orange Clan

Energy: Fiery, expressive, impulsive

Element: Fire

Symbol: The Ember Crest

Beverage: Orange Brew (citrus, electric, energising)

Role: The performers, the disruptors, the catalysts.

Clan Legacy

Energy: Balanced, evolved, future‑focused

Element: Air + Earth + Fire + Water

Symbol: The Legacy Crest

Beverage: The Zen Mixture (the fusion of Green + Orange)

Role: The future of the House — the clan Z embodies.

Clan Rituals

The traditions that define each faction.

Green Ritual — The Still Hour

A moment of silence, clarity, and grounding before creation.

Orange Ritual — The Spark Dance

A burst of movement, rhythm, and expression to ignite energy.

Legacy Ritual — The Fusion Breath

A slow inhale of Green calm + a sharp exhale of Orange fire.

The Fusion

The moment the clans become one.

The Zen Mixture

The sacred blend of Green Elixir and Orange Brew — the drink that symbolises unity, balance, and evolution.

Z’s Role

Z is the first to embody both clans fully — the living fusion, the bridge, the future.
