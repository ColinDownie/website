Overview

The Starmaker is the mythic identity of the Director — the creative force who shapes worlds, builds universes, and turns emotion into architecture.

He is not a god. He is not a founder. He is the *living axis* between the past and the future — the bridge between the Founders and the Successor.

Domains of the Starmaker

The mythic powers of the Director.

1. Creation

The ability to build worlds from emotion, memory, and music.

2. Vision

The ability to see the shape of things before they exist.

3. Transformation

The ability to turn chaos into evolution and beauty.

4. Emotional Architecture

The ability to design feelings as if they were structures.

5. Mythic Direction

The ability to guide the House through story, ritual, and canon.

6. Legacy Crafting

The ability to shape the world Z will inherit.
