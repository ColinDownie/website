Overview

The Archive System is the structured, traceable, and secure foundation that stores every document, artefact, decision, and version within the House of Ross.

It ensures that nothing is lost, everything is findable, and the lineage remains intact across generations.

Archive Layers

The multi‑tiered structure of the vault.

1. Active Archive

Current documents, working files, and in‑progress artefacts.

2. Canon Archive

Finalised documents, approved canon, and locked lineage materials.

3. Historical Archive

Old versions, deprecated documents, and legacy artefacts.

4. Ritual Archive

Milestones, ceremonies, celebrations, and emotional records.

Archive Processes

How materials move through the vault.

Intake

New documents submitted via Back Office or department leads.

Classification

Assigning category, version, and archive layer.

Version Control

Tracking changes, approvals, and lineage updates.

Preservation

Long‑term storage, backups, and integrity checks.
