Overview

Ethical Standards & Conduct define the moral expectations, behavioural principles, and cultural responsibilities of every department within the House of Ross.

These standards ensure integrity, respect, emotional intelligence, and mythic coherence across the dynasty.

Ethical Pillars

The foundational principles of conduct.

1. Integrity

Act with honesty, transparency, and consistency.

2. Respect

Treat all individuals with dignity, empathy, and fairness.

3. Responsibility

Own actions, decisions, and their impact on the House.

4. Stewardship

Protect lineage, culture, and the mythic canon.

5. Excellence

Strive for mastery in all creative and operational work.

6. Emotional Intelligence

Communicate with empathy, awareness, and cultural sensitivity.

Conduct Expectations

How members of the House must behave.

1. Communication

Speak clearly, respectfully, and in alignment with House culture.

2. Collaboration

Work cooperatively, share resources, and avoid siloing.

3. Confidentiality

Protect sensitive information and lineage‑critical knowledge.

4. Conflict Resolution

Address issues constructively and escalate appropriately.

5. Cultural Care

Support wellbeing, rituals, and emotional architecture.

6. Canon Alignment

Ensure all actions honour the mythos and House identity.

Ethical Breaches

What constitutes a violation.

1. Dishonesty

Misrepresentation, concealment, or manipulation of information.

2. Disrespect

Behaviour that harms dignity, culture, or emotional wellbeing.

3. Canon Violation

Actions that contradict lineage or mythic coherence.

4. Misuse of Resources

Improper use of tools, assets, or House property.

5. Negligence

Failure to uphold responsibilities or protect the House.

6. Cultural Harm

Behaviour that damages rituals, morale, or emotional architecture.

Enforcement

How ethical standards are upheld.

1. Department Leads

First line of accountability and correction.

2. Governance & Legal

Ensures compliance, fairness, and due process.

3. Everator

Evaluates mythic and lineage implications of breaches.

4. Muse AI

Supports emotional repair and cultural restoration.

5. Archivist AI

Records breaches, resolutions, and lineage impact.

6. Director

Final authority on major ethical decisions.
