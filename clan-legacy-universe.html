Clan Legacy Universe

Futurist Factions · Colour Logic · Unity Rituals

Overview

The Clan Legacy Universe is the futurist lineage realm — a world of factions, colours, rituals, and generational oaths. It explores how clans evolve, fracture, and reunite under shared symbols and crests.

Clan Legacy is about belonging, conflict, reconciliation, and the long arc of heritage in a modern mythic world.
