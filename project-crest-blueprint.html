Crest Construction Blueprint

Sacred Geometry of the Ross Dynasty

Overview

The Crest Construction Blueprint is the technical and symbolic guide to building any Ross House crest. It defines the geometry, ratios, layers, and emotional logic behind the dynasty’s visual identity.

Every crest — from Freedom to Everlight to Clan Legacy — follows this sacred structure.

Core Geometry

The Ross crest is built from four foundational shapes:

1 · The Core Shield

The primary silhouette. Ratio: 4:5 vertical orientation.

2 · The Ancestral Lines

Vertical + horizontal strokes dividing the crest into lineage quadrants.

3 · The Elemental Rings

Concentric circles representing Fire, Water, Air, Earth.

4 · The Mythic Crown

Top‑crest motif symbolising ascension and destiny.

Ratios & Measurements

All crests follow a strict proportional system to maintain lineage consistency:

  • Outer Shield: 100% height · 80% width
  • Inner Shield: 85% of outer shield
  • Elemental Rings: 60%, 45%, 30%, 20% of shield width
  • Ancestral Lines: 1.5% stroke weight
  • Crown Height: 18% of total crest height

Layer System

The crest is constructed in a precise layer order to ensure clarity and symbolic hierarchy:

Layer 01 · Base Shield

Defines the silhouette and emotional grounding.

Layer 02 · Elemental Rings

Represents emotional axes and elemental balance.

Layer 03 · Ancestral Lines

Marks lineage, memory, and generational continuity.

Layer 04 · Crest Glyph

The central rune or symbol defining the crest’s identity.

Layer 05 · Mythic Crown

Represents ascension, destiny, and Everlight influence.

Layer 06 · Glow & Finish

Gold accents, Everlight flares, or dual‑tone effects.
