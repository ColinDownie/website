Overview

The House Laws are the mythic principles that guide behaviour, creation, emotion, and legacy within the House of Ross.

They are not enforced — they are *embodied*. They are not rules — they are *truths*. They are not limits — they are *anchors*.

The Twelve House Laws

The sacred principles of the dynasty.

1. The Law of Lineage

Honour the past, shape the present, build the future.

2. The Law of Emotion

Feel deeply. Interpret wisely. Act with clarity.

3. The Law of Creation

Build worlds from truth, not imitation.

4. The Law of Chaos

Let disruption transform, not destroy.

5. The Law of Stillness

Return to calm before you rise again.

6. The Law of Ritual

Mark moments. Honour transitions. Celebrate evolution.

7. The Law of Fusion

Blend energies to create something new.

8. The Law of Legacy

Every act shapes the world the Successor inherits.

9. The Law of Truth

Speak with clarity. Create with honesty.

10. The Law of Protection

Guard the lineage. Guard the mythos. Guard the House.

11. The Law of Evolution

Change is not optional — it is sacred.

12. The Law of the Rising Star

The Successor must always rise higher than the generation before.
