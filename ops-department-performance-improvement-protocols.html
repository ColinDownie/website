Overview

Performance Improvement Protocols define how departments evolve based on their metrics, reviews, and lineage responsibilities.

This ensures that every division of the House of Ross grows with intention, clarity, and mythic coherence.

Improvement Triggers

When a department must evolve.

1. Metric Underperformance

Scores fall below Baseline or Standard levels.

2. Structural Misalignment

Department no longer fits the House’s evolving architecture.

3. Canon Drift

Actions or outputs stray from lineage or mythic coherence.

4. Cultural Impact

Wellbeing, morale, or emotional architecture is affected.

5. Strategic Opportunity

New possibilities emerge that require departmental evolution.

Improvement Path

The sequence for evolving a department.

Step 1: Diagnosis

Review metrics, feedback, and lineage alignment.

Step 2: Consultation

Department Lead meets with relevant departments and AI Quadrant.

Step 3: Improvement Plan

Clear goals, timelines, and responsibilities are defined.

Step 4: Implementation

Department executes the plan with cross‑department support.

Step 5: Review

Progress is evaluated using updated metrics.

Step 6: Canon Check

Everator ensures mythic and lineage coherence.

Step 7: Archive Logging

Archivist records the improvement cycle and outcomes.

Improvement Support

Who supports the evolution process.

Director

Strategist AI

Everator

Archivist AI
