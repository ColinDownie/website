Form Structure

A consistent structure across all House forms.

1. Header

Title, department, date, version.

2. Purpose

Why the form exists and when it is used.

3. Required Fields

Information that must be completed for submission.

4. Optional Fields

Additional context or supporting details.

5. Submission Path

Where the form goes and who reviews it.

6. Archive Notes

How the form is stored and referenced.
