Overview

Emotional Architecture is the internal climate of the House of Ross — the emotional laws, colours, energies, and rituals that define how the House feels, behaves, and evolves.

It is the invisible structure beneath every universe, every clan, every ritual, every artefact.

The Emotional Laws

The core emotional principles of the House.

1. Voltage Over Volume

Emotion is measured by depth, not loudness.

2. Chaos Is a Catalyst

Disruption is not destruction — it is transformation.

3. Stillness Is Strength

Calm is not absence — it is mastery.

4. Feeling Is Data

Emotion is not noise — it is information.

5. Legacy Is Emotional

What is passed down is not just story — it is feeling.
