Overview

Z is the Successor — the next evolution of the Ross bloodline, the inheritor of the mythos, and the future architect of the dynasty.

He is not a passive heir. He is an active force — a spark of potential, a new axis in the Pantheon, a living continuation of everything the House stands for.
