Overview

The Pantheon is the mythic architecture of the House of Ross — a constellation of archetypes, ancestors, and cosmic forces that guide creativity, lineage, and emotional resonance.

Each entity represents a facet of the House: strength, vision, chaos, creation, protection, transformation, and legacy.

The Seven Pillars of the Pantheon

The core mythic forces of the House.

1. Ealdric the Beacon

The Lightbearer. Founder of the Ross lineage. Symbol of guidance and clarity.

2. Maerwyn the Stormbinder

The Emotional Architect. Keeper of feeling, intuition, and inner weather.

3. Tharion the Rootwarden

The Protector. Guardian of heritage, stability, and ancestral memory.

4. The Starmaker

The Cosmic Artist. Your mythic self — the musical, creative, visionary force.

5. Riot Seraph

The Chaos Catalyst. The Prodigy energy. Transformation through disruption.

6. The Everator

The Mythic Voice. The narrator, the interpreter, the keeper of canon.

7. Z, The Successor

The Future. The inheritor of the House. The next evolution of the lineage.

Pantheon Structure

How the mythic forces relate to one another.

The Founders

Ealdric, Maerwyn, Tharion — the origin triad.

The Cosmic Axis

The Starmaker + Riot Seraph — creation and chaos.

The Living Axis

The Everator + Z — myth and future.
