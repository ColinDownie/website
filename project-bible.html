Production Bible

The Technical Spine of the Ross Dynasty

Overview

The Production Bible is the technical and procedural backbone of the House of Ross — the document set that defines how projects are built, governed, and delivered across all universes and divisions.

It turns mythic vision into repeatable practice: pipelines, standards, templates, and rules that protect quality, emotion, and lineage.

Core Chapters

01 · Foundations

Principles, definitions, and the Ross House production philosophy.

02 · Pipelines

End‑to‑end workflows for animation, design, sound, and delivery.

03 · Roles & Responsibilities

Who does what, where authority lives, and how decisions are made.

04 · Standards & Quality

Technical specs, review gates, and sign‑off rituals.

05 · Mythic Consistency

How canon, lineage, and symbolism are protected in production.

06 · Archives & Handover

File structure, naming, backups, and legacy preservation.

Relationship to Other Bibles

The Production Bible sits alongside other core documents in the Ross House Library:

  • Studio Bible: Culture, rituals, and internal mythic architecture.
  • Everlight Codex: Celestial lore and luminous rules.
  • Clan Legacy Dossier: Factions, colours, and unity logic.
  • Crest & Rune Manual: Symbol construction and usage.

Visual Language

The Production Bible uses a refined, technical aesthetic: clean grids, gold accents, parchment backgrounds, and subtle crest watermarks. It feels like a legal‑grade manual with mythic undertones.
