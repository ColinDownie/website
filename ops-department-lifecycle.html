Overview

The Department Lifecycle defines how divisions of the House of Ross are created, operated, evolved, and retired.

This ensures clarity, continuity, and mythic coherence across the entire dynasty.

Lifecycle Stages

The complete evolution of a department.

1. Conception

Intent declared by Director; purpose and lineage relevance defined.

2. Creation

Department Profile drafted using the Department Template System (17 x).

3. Activation

Governance approval, Everator blessing, and official launch.

4. Operation

Department performs its core functions and supports the House.

5. Evolution

Periodic reviews; updates to scope, systems, and canon alignment.

6. Integration

Department merges with another division if roles overlap.

7. Retirement

Department is gracefully closed; functions redistributed.

8. Archival

Archivist logs the department into the Historical Archive.

Review Cycle

How departments stay aligned with the House.

Quarterly Review

Operational performance, systems, and support relationships.

Annual Canon Review

Everator ensures narrative and lineage alignment.

AI Quadrant Audit

Strategist, Archivist, and Muse evaluate their roles and contributions.

Retirement Conditions

When a department completes its purpose.

Mission Complete

The department has fulfilled its original purpose.

Redundancy

Another department now covers the same functions.

Structural Evolution

The House reorganises and the department is no longer needed.

Director Decision

Retirement is declared with lineage respect and ceremony.
