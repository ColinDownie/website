Overview

The Ancestral Column is the mythic structure that connects every era of the House of Ross — from the Founders to the Director to the Successor and beyond.

It is not a family tree. It is a **totem** — a vertical monument of values, rituals, and emotional inheritance.

The Column Structure

The four tiers of the Ancestral Column.

Tier 1 — The Founders

Ealdric, Maerwyn, Tharion. The roots. The origin. The first flame.

Tier 2 — The Director

The Starmaker. The living architect. The one who shapes the House in the present.

Tier 3 — The Successor

Z. The rising force. The future of the lineage.

Tier 4 — The Unborn

The future generations. The ones who will inherit the rituals and symbols.
