Meeting Structure

A consistent format across the House.

1. Opening

Purpose, tone, and alignment.

2. Updates

Status, progress, and blockers.

3. Discussion

Creative, operational, or governance topics.

4. Decisions

Clear outcomes, responsibilities, and next steps.

5. Closing

Summary, emotional check‑in, and alignment.
