Overview

Audit & Review Protocols define how departments are evaluated for accuracy, alignment, performance, and compliance.

These protocols ensure transparency, quality, accountability, and mythic coherence across the House of Ross.

Audit Types

The forms of internal review.

1. Operational Audit

Evaluates workflows, systems, and execution.

2. Creative Audit

Assesses concept quality, emotional resonance, and artistic alignment.

3. Strategic Audit

Reviews long‑term alignment and structural impact.

4. Canon Audit

Ensures mythic coherence and lineage protection.

5. Cultural Audit

Evaluates wellbeing, rituals, and emotional architecture.

6. Compliance Audit

Checks adherence to legal, ethical, and governance standards.

Review Cycle

The rhythm of internal evaluation.

Quarterly Review

Operational, creative, and cultural performance.

Annual Review

Strategic alignment and structural evolution.

Canon Review

Everator evaluates mythic and lineage coherence.

Compliance Review

Governance & Legal ensures adherence to standards.

AI Quadrant Review

Strategist, Muse, and Archivist provide analysis.

Audit Path

The sequence for conducting an audit.

Step 1: Preparation

Collect documents, metrics, and lineage notes.

Step 2: Examination

Review systems, outputs, and cultural impact.

Step 3: Findings

Identify strengths, gaps, risks, and opportunities.

Step 4: Recommendations

Provide clear actions for improvement or correction.

Step 5: Response

Department acknowledges findings and commits to actions.

Step 6: Follow‑Up

Review progress and ensure completion.

Step 7: Archive Logging

Archivist records the audit and outcomes.
