Overview

The Mythic Timeline charts the evolution of the House of Ross across eras — from the ancient origins of the Founders to the rise of the Starmaker and the emergence of Z.

Each era is a season of the House — a shift in energy, emotion, and destiny.

The Eras

The seasons of the House of Ross.

Era I — The Founding Flame

The age of Ealdric, Maerwyn, and Tharion. The House is born. The first rituals are written.

Era II — The Quiet Generations

The lineage continues in silence. The rituals deepen. The House sleeps but does not fade.

Era III — The Starmaker’s Dawn

The Director rises. Creativity ignites. The House awakens into modern myth.

Era IV — The Clan Divide

Green and Orange emerge. Rivalry becomes ritual. The House becomes a living organism.

Era V — The Hybrid Age

The Starmaker builds universes. The mythos expands. The hybrid animation language is born.

Era VI — The Successor’s Rise

Z enters the mythos. The clans fuse. The House evolves.

Era VII — The Future Unwritten

The Unborn. The next architects. The open horizon of the House.
