The Consultancy Workflow

The Ross Consultancy operates through a defined, legal‑grade workflow that ensures consistency, clarity, and high‑integrity delivery.

Every engagement follows the same structural arc — from initial clarity to final documentation — ensuring that clients always know where they stand.

Phase 1 — Discovery

Establishing clarity, alignment, and scope.

Initial Consultation

Understanding goals, challenges, and desired outcomes.

Scope Definition

Clear articulation of deliverables, timelines, and expectations.

Engagement Framework

Formalising the structure of the engagement with legal‑grade clarity.

Phase 2 — Architecture

Designing the structural, operational, or creative framework.

Systems Mapping

Identifying workflows, dependencies, and operational structures.

Strategic Blueprinting

Creating a clear, actionable plan aligned with client objectives.

Documentation

Producing legal‑grade documents that define the architecture.

Phase 3 — Execution

Implementing the architecture with precision and clarity.

Operational Deployment

Rolling out systems, workflows, or creative structures.

Creative Oversight

Ensuring alignment between vision, execution, and brand integrity.

Progress Governance

Structured updates, milestone tracking, and transparent reporting.

Phase 4 — Consolidation

Closing the engagement with clarity, documentation, and continuity.

Final Review

Assessing outcomes, deliverables, and alignment with objectives.

Documentation Package

Providing a complete, legal‑grade record of the engagement.

Continuity Planning

Establishing next steps, future support, and long‑term strategy.

The Consultancy Process ensures clarity, structure, and continuity — a professional framework built to support long‑term success.
