Archive Division

Mandate

The Archive Division preserves the living memory of Ross House. Every textile, avatar, crest, and timeline is protected as part of the dynasty’s evolving history.

Responsibilities

  • Textile Records & Fabric Histories
  • Avatar Lineage & Mythic Profiles
  • Crest Evolution & Heraldry
  • Universe Timelines & Constellation Maps
  • Legacy Asset Preservation

Archive Ritual

Every artefact enters the Archive through ceremony. Textiles are catalogued with lineage markers. Avatars are recorded with emotional signatures. Crests are logged with generational metadata.
