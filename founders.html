Overview

The Three Founders are the mythic ancestors of the House of Ross — the first keepers of lineage, the original architects of strength, emotion, and memory.

They are not gods. They are not spirits. They are the *archetypal forces* that shaped the Ross bloodline.

The Founders

The origin triad and their domains.

Ealdric the Beacon

Domain: Guidance, clarity, vision

Symbol: The Lantern Crest

Rune: A vertical line splitting light and shadow

Role: The one who sees the path before others.

Maerwyn the Stormbinder

Domain: Emotion, intuition, inner weather

Symbol: The Spiral Storm

Rune: A swirling knot of wind and heart

Role: The one who shapes feeling into strength.

Tharion the Rootwarden

Domain: Protection, heritage, memory

Symbol: The Rooted Shield

Rune: A branching tree descending into earth

Role: The one who guards the lineage.
