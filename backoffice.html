Back Office

Mandate

The Back Office is the operational heart of Ross House — the wing responsible for governance, documentation, systems, emotional architecture, and the preservation of institutional memory. It ensures continuity, structure, and ceremonial order across the dynasty.

Divisions

  • Archive Division — Memory, artefacts, lineage records
  • Governance — Policy, oversight, internal law
  • Documentation — Manuals, protocols, written canon
  • Systems — Operational frameworks, workflows, infrastructure
  • Emotional Architecture — Rituals, tone, internal culture

Purpose

This wing exists to maintain the integrity of the House. Where the Pantheon shapes myth and the Consultancy shapes leadership, the Back Office shapes **continuity** — the quiet machinery that keeps the dynasty coherent.

Leadership

The Back Office is overseen by the Director of Operations, supported by archivists, system architects, and ceremonial officers who maintain the House’s internal order.
