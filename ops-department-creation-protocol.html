Overview

The Department Creation Protocol ensures that every new division of the House of Ross is born with clarity, purpose, structure, and mythic coherence.

This protocol defines the steps, approvals, templates, and rituals required to establish a new department within the dynasty.

Creation Steps

The sequence for birthing a new department.

1. Intent Declaration

Director defines the purpose, scope, and lineage relevance.

2. Drafting the Department Profile

Using the Department Template System (17 x) to create the first version.

3. Governance Review

Compliance, risk, and operational alignment checks.

4. Canon Alignment

Everator ensures mythic and narrative coherence.

5. AI Quadrant Integration

Strategist, Archivist, and Muse define their roles within the new department.

6. Approval Gate

Director signs off; Archivist logs the creation event.

7. Department Activation

Page is created, added to the Department Index, and announced.
