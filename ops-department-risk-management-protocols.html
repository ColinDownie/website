Overview

Risk Management Protocols define how departments identify, assess, mitigate, and prevent risks that could impact the House of Ross.

These protocols ensure safety, continuity, emotional intelligence, and mythic coherence across the dynasty.

Risk Categories

The core dimensions of risk.

1. Operational Risks

Workflow failures, delays, resource shortages, system breakdowns.

2. Creative Risks

Concept drift, loss of emotional resonance, misaligned outputs.

3. Strategic Risks

Misalignment with long‑term goals or structural evolution.

4. Cultural Risks

Wellbeing issues, morale drops, emotional architecture disruption.

5. Canon Risks

Contradictions to lineage, mythos, or narrative coherence.

6. Compliance Risks

Legal, ethical, or governance violations.

Risk Identification

How risks are discovered.

1. Metrics Review

Performance data reveals emerging issues.

2. Department Feedback

Teams report concerns, patterns, or anomalies.

3. AI Quadrant Alerts

Strategist, Everator, Archivist, or Muse detect misalignment.

4. Cross‑Department Signals

Collaboration reveals friction or structural gaps.

5. Director Oversight

Top‑level review identifies systemic risks.

Risk Mitigation

How risks are reduced or neutralised.

1. Immediate Action

Quick fixes to prevent escalation.

2. Structural Adjustment

Workflow, resource, or role changes.

3. Canon Correction

Everator restores mythic and lineage coherence.

4. Emotional Stabilisation

Muse AI supports wellbeing and cultural balance.

5. Compliance Intervention

Governance & Legal resolves legal or ethical issues.

Risk Prevention

How risks are avoided entirely.

1. Regular Reviews

Quarterly, annual, and canon reviews.

2. Training & Development

Skill‑building and emotional intelligence training.

3. Clear Documentation

Transparent processes and accessible records.

4. Cross‑Department Collaboration

Shared knowledge reduces blind spots.

5. AI Quadrant Monitoring

Continuous analysis from Strategist, Muse, Everator, Archivist.
