The Great Index Overview

Book XXIV — The Great Index

Purpose

The Great Index is the master reference of the Ross Universe. It unifies all books, concepts, clans, realms, forces, rituals, and mythic structures into a single navigable system. This is the codex used by archivists, storytellers, ritual leaders, and future generations of the lineage.

Structure

The Index is divided into three major sections:

  • Universal Index — every major concept across all 24 books.
  • Cross‑References — links between related ideas, forces, and entities.
  • Meta‑Notes — commentary on structure, lineage, and mythic architecture.

This book is the final anchor of the Masterbook.

Notes

Future expansions may include search systems, glyph‑based indexing, and digital cross‑realm navigation.
