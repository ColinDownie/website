Clan Attire

Book XXI — Fashion

Introduction

Clan Attire reflects the emotional and cultural identity of each energy type. Silhouettes, colours, and materials shift depending on lineage, ritual purpose, and personal expression.

Main Content

Core Clan Attire includes:

  • Green Attire — soft fabrics, layered silhouettes, muted tones, functional elegance.
  • Orange Attire — sharp cuts, bright accents, asymmetry, expressive textures.
  • Zen Attire — mirrored garments, dual‑tone fabrics, balanced shapes, flowing lines.
  • Seraphic Attire — fractured patterns, fire‑edge trims, volatile silhouettes, bold contrasts.

These garments define how clans present themselves in daily life and ceremonial contexts.

Notes

Future expansions may include clan wardrobes, seasonal variations, and accessory systems.
