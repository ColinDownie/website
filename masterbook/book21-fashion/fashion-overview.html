Fashion Overview

Book XXI — Fashion

Introduction

Fashion in the Ross Universe is more than clothing — it is lineage, symbolism, emotional architecture, and clan identity made physical. Every garment carries meaning, ritual purpose, and mythic resonance.

Main Content

This book introduces three major fashion layers:

  • Clan Attire — the everyday and ceremonial clothing of Green, Orange, Zen, and Seraphic identities.
  • Ritual Garments — clothing worn during vigils, oaths, convergences, and mythic ceremonies.
  • Mythic Textiles — legendary fabrics, patterns, and materials tied to the Forebears and cosmic forces.

Together, these layers form the visual and tactile identity of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include textile diagrams, colour palettes, and garment construction notes.
