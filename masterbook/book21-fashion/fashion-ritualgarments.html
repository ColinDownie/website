Ritual Garments

Book XXI — Fashion

Introduction

Ritual Garments are worn during ceremonies, vigils, oaths, and mythic transitions. These garments carry symbolic weight and emotional purpose, often tied to lineage or cosmic alignment.

Main Content

Key Ritual Garments include:

  • The Vigil Cloak — a long, soft garment worn during remembrance rituals.
  • The Oathweave Sash — a woven band symbolising commitment and lineage.
  • The Convergence Robe — dual‑coloured fabric representing unity and balance.
  • The Seraphic Mantle — a fire‑trimmed garment worn during transformative rites.
  • The Beacon Shawl — a luminous wrap used in guidance and clarity ceremonies.

These garments shape the visual language of ritual and mythic practice.
