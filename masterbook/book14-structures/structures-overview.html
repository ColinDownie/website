Structures Overview

Book XIV — Structures

Introduction

Structures are the built environments of the Ross Universe — ancestral halls, ritual sites, clan dwellings, and mythic architecture. These spaces shape behaviour, memory, and emotional resonance. Every structure carries lineage, purpose, and symbolic meaning.

Main Content

This book introduces three major categories of structures:

  • Halls — central clan buildings tied to lineage, gatherings, and governance.
  • Sites — ritual locations where ceremonies, oaths, and transitions occur.
  • Sanctuaries — protected spaces for reflection, healing, and inner work.

Together, these structures form the architectural backbone of the Ross Universe — grounding its culture in physical form.

Notes

Future expansions may include architectural diagrams, floor plans, and construction histories.
