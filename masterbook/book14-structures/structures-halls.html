Halls

Book XIV — Structures

Introduction

Halls are the central architectural anchors of the clans. They serve as gathering places, decision chambers, and repositories of lineage. Each hall reflects the emotional and cultural identity of the clan it belongs to.

Main Content

Key Halls include:

  • The Ancestral Hall — a chamber of portraits, journals, and heirlooms honouring past generations.
  • The Council Hall — a circular room for governance, debate, and clan decision‑making.
  • The Keeper’s Archive — a quiet hall of lanterns, scrolls, and memory artefacts.
  • The Dual Clans Forum — a shared hall where Green and Orange once reconciled.
  • The Starmaker Atrium — an open, resonant space used for music‑driven ceremonies.

These halls form the civic and emotional centres of the Ross Universe’s built environment.

Notes

Future expansions may include hall layouts, architectural motifs, and ceremonial uses.
