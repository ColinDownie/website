Sanctuaries

Book XIV — Structures

Introduction

Sanctuaries are protected spaces for reflection, healing, creativity, and inner transformation. These structures are quieter, more intimate, and deeply tied to emotional architecture.

Main Content

Key Sanctuaries include:

  • The Quiet Chamber — a sound‑softened room for meditation and grounding.
  • The Creative Nook — a small sanctuary for writing, drawing, and artistic flow.
  • The Memory Garden — an enclosed courtyard for reflection and ancestral connection.
  • The Harmony Alcove — a dual‑coloured space symbolising balance and reconciliation.
  • The Stormwatch Retreat — a cliffside sanctuary for processing change and emotional release.

These sanctuaries form the emotional interior of the Ross Universe’s architecture.

Notes

Future expansions may include sanctuary layouts, sensory design notes, and emotional use cases.
