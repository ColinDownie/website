Sites

Book XIV — Structures

Introduction

Sites are ritual locations — places where transitions, ceremonies, and generational moments unfold. These spaces hold emotional charge and symbolic purpose, often tied to myths or ancestral events.

Main Content

Key Sites include:

  • The Oathstone Circle — where vows of stewardship and unity are spoken.
  • The Lantern Path — a walkway lit during remembrance rituals.
  • The Successor’s Platform — a raised stone used for rites of passage.
  • The Echo Arch — a resonant stone archway tied to prophecy and reflection.
  • The Zen Vessel Shrine — a sacred site honouring the merging of dual energies.

These sites shape the ceremonial rhythm of the Ross Universe.
