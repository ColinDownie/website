Natural Creatures

Book XII — Creatures

Introduction

Natural Creatures are the fauna that populate the realms of the Ross Universe. These animals form the ecological foundation of the world — shaping landscapes, influencing culture, and grounding the mythic elements in a sense of reality.

Main Content

Key Natural Creatures include:

  • Highland Stags — graceful, resilient animals symbolising vigilance and quiet strength.
  • River Otters — playful, social creatures associated with curiosity and emotional ease.
  • Sky Kestrels — swift birds known for their precision and far‑reaching vision.
  • Glen Hounds — loyal, protective companions often found near clan settlements.
  • Moor Hares — quick, alert animals tied to intuition and seasonal change.

These creatures give the Ross Universe its texture — grounding the mythic and symbolic layers in a living, breathing environment.

Notes

Future expansions may include habitat maps, behavioural notes, and cross‑realm migrations.
