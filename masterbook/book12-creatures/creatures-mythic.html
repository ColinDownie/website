Mythic Creatures

Book XII — Creatures

Introduction

Mythic Creatures are legendary beings tied to the deepest stories and cosmic forces of the Ross Universe. These creatures embody ancient truths, emotional archetypes, and the power of the Forebears. They appear in myths, visions, rituals, and generational lore.

Main Content

Key Mythic Creatures include:

  • The Beacon Stag — a radiant, otherworldly hart associated with Ealdric the Beacon and moments of guidance.
  • The Stormbinder Mare — a spectral horse that rides the edges of storms, tied to Maerwyn’s command of chaos.
  • The Rootwarden Bear — a colossal guardian spirit representing Tharion’s grounding strength and ancestral memory.
  • The Zen Serpent — a luminous, dual‑coloured serpent symbolising unity, balance, and the merging of Green and Orange.
  • The Riot Seraph’s Shadow — a winged, shifting creature of fire and distortion, heralding transformation and upheaval.

These mythic beings shape the spiritual and emotional landscape of the Ross Universe — influencing rituals, guiding characters, and echoing through the dynasty’s deepest stories.

Notes

Future expansions may include illustrated bestiaries, mythic encounters, and cross‑realm manifestations.
