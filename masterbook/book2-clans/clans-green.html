Green Faction

Book II — Clans

Introduction

The Green Faction embodies calm, clarity, and grounded emotional intelligence. They are the Clan of reflection, balance, and measured action — the steadying force within the wider mythos.

Main Content

Their colours, symbols, and rituals reflect growth, renewal, and the quiet strength of lineage. The Green Faction often serves as the emotional anchor during inter‑clan disputes or transitions.

Their beverage blend is herbal, cooling, and restorative — a counterbalance to the fiery Orange Faction.

Notes

Future expansions may include Green Faction artefacts, character archetypes, and ritual sequences.
