Orange Faction

Book II — Clans

Introduction

The Orange Faction embodies energy, momentum, and creative fire. They are the Clan of action, disruption, and emotional voltage — the spark that drives transformation within the Ross House mythos.

Main Content

Their colours, symbols, and rituals reflect heat, passion, and the boldness required to break old patterns. The Orange Faction often catalyses change, pushing the other Clans into evolution.

Their beverage blend is bright, citrus‑charged, and invigorating — the perfect counterpoint to the calming Green Faction.

Notes

Future expansions may include Orange Faction artefacts, character archetypes, and ritual sequences.
