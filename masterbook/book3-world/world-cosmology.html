World Cosmology

Book III — World

Introduction

The cosmology of the Ross House world defines the forces that shape existence — lineage, emotional laws, mythic archetypes, and the unseen energies that guide the evolution of the dynasty.

Main Content

The cosmos is structured around three mythic layers:

  • The Lineage Sphere — the ancestral realm where the Ross forebears reside.
  • The Creative Ether — the plane where ideas, stories, and avatars take form.
  • The Mortal World — the lived landscape shaped by emotion, ritual, and legacy.

These layers interact constantly. Inspiration descends from the Creative Ether, lineage rises from the ancestral sphere, and the mortal world becomes the stage where both forces meet.

Notes

Future expansions may include celestial maps, pantheon structures, and mythic cycles.
