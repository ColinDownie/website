World Laws

Book III — World

Introduction

The World Laws define the emotional physics of the Ross Universe. These laws govern how lineage flows, how creativity manifests, and how mythic forces interact across the world’s regions and realms.

Main Content

Core laws include:

  • The Law of Lineage — all creation is rooted in ancestry and legacy.
  • The Law of Duality — opposing forces (calm and fire, stillness and motion) must coexist for balance to emerge.
  • The Law of Emotional Resonance — every action carries emotional weight that shapes the world around it.
  • The Law of Creative Echo — ideas born in the Creative Ether ripple through the mortal world until they take form.
  • The Law of Return — all stories, energies, and creations eventually circle back to their origin.

These laws are not rigid. They evolve as the dynasty evolves, adapting to new stories, new avatars, and new emotional truths.

Notes

Future expansions may include advanced emotional laws, mythic exceptions, and lineage‑specific rules.
