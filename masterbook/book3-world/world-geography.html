World Geography

Book III — World

Introduction

The geography of the Ross House world is shaped by emotional architecture. Regions are not defined by borders alone, but by the feelings, histories, and mythic energies that flow through them.

Main Content

Key geographical zones include:

  • The Lineage Plains — open lands symbolising heritage and continuity.
  • The Crest Mountains — towering peaks representing legacy and endurance.
  • The Studio District — the creative capital where ideas take physical form.
  • The Clan Territories — emotional zones shaped by Green and Orange energies.
  • The Zen Valley — the sacred meeting point where duality becomes balance.

Each region carries its own symbolism, rituals, and narrative weight. Geography in this world is a living map of the dynasty’s emotional evolution.

Notes

Future expansions may include illustrated maps, travel routes, and mythic landmarks.
