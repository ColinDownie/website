Symbols

Book XI — Artefacts

Introduction

Symbols are artefacts that represent the values, clans, and emotional states of the Ross dynasty. These objects serve as visual anchors — instantly recognisable emblems that communicate identity, unity, and mythic meaning.

Main Content

Key Symbols include:

  • The Dual Crest — the unified emblem of Green and Orange, representing balance and harmony.
  • The Zen Circle — a symbol of resolution, stillness, and the merging of dual energies.
  • The Clan Marks — geometric icons representing Green calm and Orange fire.
  • The Lineage Spiral — a symbol of generational continuity and ancestral resonance.
  • The Creative Sigil — an emblem tied to inspiration, breakthrough, and artistic flow.

These symbols appear across rituals, garments, artefacts, and architectural elements — forming the visual language of the Ross Universe.
