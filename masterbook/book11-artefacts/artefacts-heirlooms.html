Heirlooms

Book XI — Artefacts

Introduction

Heirlooms are the most intimate artefacts of the Ross dynasty. These objects carry memory, emotion, and generational continuity. They are passed down not for their material value, but for the stories and resonance they hold.

Main Content

Key Heirlooms include:

  • The Lineage Ring — a symbol of stewardship worn by protectors and successors.
  • The Ancestral Journal — a handwritten record of reflections, lessons, and emotional truths.
  • The Clan Pendants — Green and Orange emblems passed down through family branches.
  • The Memory Box — a container for personal relics, letters, and emotional artefacts.
  • The Keeper’s Key — a symbolic object representing guardianship of the House’s inner chambers.

These heirlooms form the emotional backbone of the dynasty — grounding characters in their history and connecting each generation to the next.

Notes

Future expansions may include heirloom illustrations, provenance charts, and cross‑generational stories.
