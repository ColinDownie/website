Mythic Artefacts

Book XI — Artefacts

Introduction

Mythic Artefacts are legendary objects tied to the deepest stories of the Ross Universe. These items hold symbolic power, cosmic resonance, and connections to the Forebears, the Clans, and the forces that shape the dynasty’s fate.

Main Content

Key Mythic Artefacts include:

  • The Beacon Staff — the radiant artefact of Ealdric the Beacon, symbolising guidance and clarity.
  • The Stormbinder’s Bridle — Maerwyn’s legendary tool for calming chaos and directing elemental force.
  • The Rootwarden Stone — Tharion’s grounding relic, tied to protection, memory, and ancestral strength.
  • The Zen Vessel — the mythic cup that unites Green and Orange energies into perfect harmony.
  • The Seraph Shard — a fragment of the Riot Seraph’s disruptive power, used to ignite transformation.

These artefacts appear in myths, rituals, and narrative cycles — shaping the destiny of characters and influencing the emotional evolution of the House.

Notes

Future expansions may include artefact illustrations, origin stories, and cross‑realm interactions.
