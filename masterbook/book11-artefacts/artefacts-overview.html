Artefacts Overview

Book XI — Artefacts

Introduction

Artefacts are the physical embodiments of the Ross dynasty’s history, memory, and myth. These objects carry emotional resonance, symbolic meaning, and generational significance. Some are everyday tools elevated by lineage; others are mythic relics tied to the universe’s deepest stories.

Main Content

This chapter introduces the three major categories of artefacts:

  • Heirlooms — objects passed down through generations, carrying memory and legacy.
  • Symbols — items that represent values, clans, or emotional states within the dynasty.
  • Mythic Artefacts — legendary objects tied to the Forebears, the Clans, or cosmic forces.

Together, these artefacts form the tangible backbone of the Ross Universe — grounding its stories, rituals, and characters in physical reality.

Notes

Future expansions may include illustrated artefacts, crafting histories, and cross‑generational provenance charts.
