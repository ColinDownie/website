Institutions

Book XXIII — Society

Introduction

Institutions maintain order, preserve lineage, and guide the emotional and cultural development of the Ross Universe. These bodies hold authority, memory, and long‑term responsibility.

Main Content

Key Institutions include:

  • The Council of Clans — the primary decision‑making body.
  • The Lineage Archive — the keeper of ancestral records and artefacts.
  • The Vigil Order — a group dedicated to emotional care and remembrance.
  • The Convergence Court — mediators of conflict and dual‑energy disputes.
  • The Seraphic Watch — guardians monitoring volatile forces and anomalies.

These institutions form the structural backbone of Ross Universe society.
