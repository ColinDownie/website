Society Overview

Book XXIII — Society

Introduction

Society in the Ross Universe is shaped by lineage, clan identity, emotional architecture, and mythic influence. Communities form around shared values, rituals, and the forces that guide their lives. Social structures reflect balance, tension, and the ongoing dance between Green, Orange, Zen, and Seraphic energies.

Main Content

This book introduces three major social layers:

  • Communities — groups formed by clan ties, geography, or shared purpose.
  • Traditions — recurring practices that shape identity and belonging.
  • Institutions — structured organisations that maintain order, memory, and cultural continuity.

Together, these layers form the living social fabric of the Ross Universe.
