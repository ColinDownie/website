Communities

Book XXIII — Society

Introduction

Communities form the everyday social structure of the Ross Universe. They arise from clan ties, shared work, emotional alignment, or geographic proximity. Each community carries its own rhythm, values, and internal dynamics.

Main Content

Key Community Types include:

  • Clan Villages — settlements shaped by Green or Orange identity.
  • Zen Enclaves — balanced communities built around unity and reflection.
  • Crafting Circles — groups dedicated to textiles, tools, or myth‑tech creation.
  • Vigil Houses — supportive communities centred on remembrance and emotional care.
  • Wanderer Bands — nomadic groups moving between realms and thresholds.

These communities define how people live, collaborate, and support one another.

Notes

Future expansions may include community layouts, social roles, and inter‑clan dynamics.
