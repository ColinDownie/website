Traditions

Book XXIII — Society

Introduction

Traditions are the recurring practices that bind communities together. They preserve memory, reinforce identity, and create emotional continuity across generations.

Main Content

Key Traditions include:

  • The Lantern Vigil — a night of remembrance and quiet unity.
  • The Dual Crossing — a symbolic journey between Green and Orange territories.
  • The Starmaker Festival — a celebration of creativity, music, and lineage.
  • The Rootwarden Feast — a gathering honouring ancestry and shared memory.
  • The Seraphic Break Rite — a ritual marking transformation and renewal.

These traditions shape the cultural rhythm of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include seasonal calendars, ceremonial scripts, and regional variations.
