Regions

Book XIII — Landscapes

Introduction

Regions are specific areas within realms — places with focused stories, rituals, and clan associations. They are where the broad emotional tone of a realm becomes intimate and personal.

Main Content

Key Regions include:

  • Keeper’s Glen — a secluded valley tied to the Keeper’s Vigil, lantern rituals, and remembrance.
  • Successor’s Ridge — a high overlook where oaths of stewardship and succession are spoken.
  • The Dual Clans Crossing — a bridge and ford where Green and Orange once divided and later reconciled.
  • Echo Loch — a still body of water known for reflecting more than just the visible world.
  • The Starmaker Steps — a terraced ascent used for music‑driven ceremonies and creative awakenings.

These regions provide narrative anchors — recognisable locations where key scenes, memories, and generational moments unfold.

Notes

Future expansions may include regional maps, landmark lists, and ritual route diagrams.
