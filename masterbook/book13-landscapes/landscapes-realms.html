Realms

Book XIII — Landscapes

Introduction

Realms are the largest environmental structures in the Ross Universe. Each realm carries a distinct emotional tone, mythic function, and visual character. They are the stage on which clans, creatures, and narratives unfold.

Main Content

Key Realms include:

  • The Highland Crown — a realm of ridges, lochs, and open skies, associated with vigilance and clarity.
  • The Riverwoven Vale — a lush, flowing landscape of rivers and glens, tied to memory and emotional ease.
  • The Stormline Coast — a dramatic shoreline of cliffs and waves, linked to change, tension, and release.
  • The Rootbound Woods — dense forests and ancient trees, connected to ancestry, grounding, and quiet strength.
  • The Skybridge Plateau — elevated plains and wind‑carved stone, associated with vision, perspective, and decision.

These realms form the macro‑geography of the Ross Universe — each one a distinct emotional climate that shapes the stories told within it.

Notes

Future expansions may include realm maps, climate notes, and cross‑realm travel routes.
