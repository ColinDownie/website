Technology Overview

Book XXII — Technology

Introduction

Technology in the Ross Universe blends craftsmanship, emotional architecture, and mythic forces. Tools and systems are not merely functional — they carry lineage, symbolism, and ritual purpose. Every device reflects the values of the clans and the cosmic forces that shape the world.

Main Content

This book introduces three major technological layers:

  • Tools & Devices — crafted objects used in daily life and ritual practice.
  • Systems & Interfaces — organisational and communicative technologies.
  • Myth‑Tech — hybrid creations powered by cosmic forces and emotional energy.

Together, these layers form the engineered identity of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include schematics, interface diagrams, and myth‑tech blueprints.
