Introduction

Tools and devices in the Ross Universe blend craftsmanship with emotional and symbolic purpose. They are used in daily life, clan work, and ritual practice.

Main Content

Key Tools & Devices include:

  • The Vigil Lantern — a memory‑holding light used in remembrance rituals.
  • The Oathstone Chisel — a tool for carving lineage marks and commitments.
  • The Dual Compass — a device that aligns with both Green and Orange energies.
  • The Stormbinder Gauge — a tension‑measuring instrument used in conflict mediation.
  • The Beacon Lens — a clarity‑enhancing optical tool used for guidance work.

These tools reflect the craftsmanship and emotional intelligence of the clans.
