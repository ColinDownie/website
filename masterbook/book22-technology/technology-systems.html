Systems & Interfaces

Book XXII — Technology

Introduction

Systems and interfaces organise communication, decision‑making, and emotional flow. These technologies reflect the clans’ values of clarity, balance, and lineage.

Main Content

Key Systems & Interfaces include:

  • The Lineage Archive — a structured system for storing ancestral records.
  • The Convergence Board — a dual‑energy interface for conflict resolution.
  • The Vigil Network — a communication system for remembrance and support.
  • The Stormbinder Protocol — a structured method for managing tension and change.
  • The Beacon Grid — a guidance‑mapping interface used for navigation and planning.

These systems form the operational backbone of the Ross Universe.
