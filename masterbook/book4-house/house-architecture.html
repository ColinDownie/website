House Architecture

Book IV — House

Introduction

The architecture of the Ross House is both literal and symbolic. Every room, corridor, and division reflects an emotional truth, a lineage principle, or a creative function within the dynasty.

Main Content

Key architectural elements include:

  • The Lineage Hall — the central chamber where ancestry is honoured.
  • The Studio Wing — the creative engine of the House, filled with tools, artefacts, and works‑in‑progress.
  • The Clan Chambers — spaces shaped by Green and Orange energies, each with its own emotional architecture.
  • The Zen Atrium — a place of balance, reflection, and ritual unity.
  • The Observatory — the room that connects the House to the cosmology above.

The House grows as the dynasty grows. New rooms appear as new stories, rituals, and creative practices emerge.
