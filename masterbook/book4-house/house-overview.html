House Overview

Book IV — House

Introduction

The Ross House is the structural heart of the dynasty — a place of lineage, creativity, governance, and emotional architecture. It is both a literal home and a mythic construct, shaped by the values and rituals of the family.

Main Content

This chapter introduces the House as a living entity: its divisions, its symbolic rooms, its emotional layout, and the principles that guide its operation.

The House is where lineage becomes structure, where creativity becomes tradition, and where every story finds its anchor.
