House Rituals

Book IV — House

Introduction

Rituals are the emotional spine of the Ross House. They mark transitions, honour lineage, and reinforce the values that hold the dynasty together. Every ritual is both symbolic and practical — a moment where creativity, emotion, and heritage converge.

Main Content

Core House rituals include:

  • The Lineage Invocation — a ceremony honouring ancestors before major creative acts.
  • The Studio Ignition — the ritual that opens new creative cycles or projects.
  • The Clan Balance Rite — a symbolic act ensuring Green and Orange energies remain aligned.
  • The Zen Pour — the blending of the two Clan beverages into the sacred mixture of unity.
  • The Archive Sealing — the ritual of closing a chapter, project, or era within the House.

These rituals give rhythm to the dynasty. They transform everyday actions into moments of meaning, grounding the House in tradition while allowing it to evolve.

Notes

Future expansions may include seasonal rituals, family ceremonies, and creative rites of passage.
