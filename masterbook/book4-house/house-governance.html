House Governance

Book IV — House

Introduction

Governance is the structural backbone of the Ross House. It defines how decisions are made, how lineage is protected, and how the emotional and creative integrity of the dynasty is upheld.

Main Content

The House operates through a layered governance model:

  • The Lineage Council — guardians of heritage, precedent, and ancestral law.
  • The Studio Directorate — leaders of creative output, innovation, and artistic evolution.
  • The Clan Representatives — voices of emotional balance, ensuring Green and Orange energies remain aligned.
  • The Keeper of Rituals — custodian of ceremonies, transitions, and symbolic acts.
  • The Archivist — protector of documents, artefacts, and the Masterbook itself.

Governance in the Ross House is not rigid. It adapts as the dynasty grows, ensuring that structure never suffocates creativity or emotional truth.

Future expansions may include charters, role descriptions, and governance rituals.
