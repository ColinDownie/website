Outer Realms

Book XVIII — Realms Beyond

Introduction

Outer Realms lie at the far edges of existence — vast, distant planes shaped by cosmic forces and ancient mythic cycles. These realms are rarely visited, but their influence reaches inward.

Main Content

Key Outer Realms include:

  • The Far Beacon Expanse — a luminous void where guidance and revelation originate.
  • The Tempest Fringe — a storm‑wracked borderland of shifting winds and raw chaos.
  • The Deeproot Beyond — a subterranean cosmic layer tied to ancient memory.
  • The Horizon Divide — a distant plane where dual energies drift in parallel.
  • The Seraphic Outer Rift — a volatile frontier of fire, distortion, and rebirth.

These realms define the outer boundaries of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include outer‑realm maps, travel myths, and cosmic navigation rules.
