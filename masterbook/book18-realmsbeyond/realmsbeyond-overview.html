Realms Beyond Overview

Book XVIII — Realms Beyond

Introduction

Realms Beyond are the outer layers of existence — dreamlike territories, hidden dimensions, and unreachable spaces that lie outside the known world. These realms influence prophecy, transformation, and the deepest mythic cycles of the Ross Universe.

Main Content

This book introduces three major categories of beyond-realms:

  • Outer Realms — distant planes that border the known universe.
  • Hidden Realms — subtle layers that overlap with the physical world.
  • Forbidden Realms — unstable or dangerous spaces touched by anomaly and myth.

These realms expand the cosmology and deepen the mystery of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include travel rules, planar maps, and cross‑realm interactions.
