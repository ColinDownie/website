Forbidden Realms

Book XVIII — Realms Beyond

Introduction

Forbidden Realms are unstable, dangerous, or cosmically distorted spaces. They are shaped by anomaly, mythic rupture, or ancient events that left the realm altered. Few enter — fewer return unchanged.

Main Content

Key Forbidden Realms include:

  • The Shattered Beacon Field — a fractured realm of broken light and lost guidance.
  • The Stormbinder Maw — a vortex of chaotic winds and looping time.
  • The Rootwarden Hollow — a collapsed memory‑realm where echoes devour themselves.
  • The Dual Rupture — a realm torn between Green and Orange, unstable and unpredictable.
  • The Seraphic Abyss — a deep, fiery void tied to transformation, destruction, and rebirth.

These forbidden realms mark the edges of myth, danger, and cosmic instability.

Notes

Future expansions may include anomaly maps, forbidden‑realm warnings, and mythic case studies.
