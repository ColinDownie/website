Hidden Realms

Book XVIII — Realms Beyond

Introduction

Hidden Realms overlap with the physical world but remain unseen without ritual, vision, or emotional alignment. These subtle layers influence intuition, memory, and symbolic meaning.

Main Content

Key Hidden Realms include:

  • The Lantern Veil — a realm of soft light where memories drift like echoes.
  • The Whispering Glen — a hidden layer of forests where thoughts and instincts sharpen.
  • The Echo Mirror — a reflective realm where inner truths appear as shifting images.
  • The Dual Trace — a subtle plane where Green and Orange energies leave emotional imprints.
  • The Quiet Fold — a realm of stillness accessed during deep reflection or grief.

These hidden realms shape intuition, symbolism, and emotional resonance.

Notes

Future expansions may include access rituals, emotional triggers, and cross‑realm overlays.
