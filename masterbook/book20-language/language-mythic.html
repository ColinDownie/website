Mythic Terms

Book XX — Language

Introduction

Mythic Terms are ancient words tied to prophecy, cosmic cycles, and the Forebears. These terms appear in visions, rituals, and the deepest layers of the Masterbook.

Main Content

Key Mythic Terms include:

  • Astra‑Vigil — a celestial moment of remembrance.
  • Chrono‑Rift — a temporal break tied to destiny shifts.
  • Harmonic Convergence — the alignment of dual energies.
  • Seraphic Ascension — a transformative rise through fire.
  • Root‑Echo — an ancestral message carried through time.
  • Beaconfall — the moment guidance collapses into revelation.

These mythic terms deepen the linguistic and symbolic architecture of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include prophecy scripts, ancient glyphs, and cross‑realm translations.
