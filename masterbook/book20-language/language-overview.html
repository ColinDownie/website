Language Overview

Book XX — Language

Introduction

Language is the connective tissue of the Ross Universe. It shapes identity, ritual, memory, and myth. Words carry emotional weight, lineage, and symbolic meaning. Every clan, realm, and mythic force expresses itself through distinct linguistic patterns.

Main Content

This book introduces three major linguistic layers:

  • The Core Lexicon — foundational words, names, and concepts.
  • Clan Dialects — the speech patterns of Green, Orange, Zen, and Seraphic energies.
  • Mythic Terms — ancient words tied to prophecy, lineage, and cosmic forces.

Together, these layers form the spoken and written identity of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include glyphs, pronunciation guides, and ritual scripts.
