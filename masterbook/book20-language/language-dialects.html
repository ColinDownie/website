Clan Dialects

Book XX — Language

Introduction

Each clan and energy type expresses itself through distinct linguistic patterns. Dialects reflect emotional states, cultural values, and mythic alignment.

Main Content

Core Dialects include:

  • Green Dialect — calm phrasing, steady cadence, reflective vocabulary.
  • Orange Dialect — sharp verbs, urgent rhythm, expressive emphasis.
  • Zen Dialect — balanced structure, mirrored phrases, dual‑layered meaning.
  • Seraphic Dialect — fragmented syntax, fire‑edge metaphors, volatile imagery.

These dialects shape how clans speak, negotiate, and perform rituals.
