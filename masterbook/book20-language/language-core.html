The Core Lexicon

Book XX — Language

Introduction

The Core Lexicon contains the foundational words that define the Ross Universe. These terms appear across rituals, stories, structures, and mythic cycles. Each word carries emotional and symbolic resonance.

Main Content

Key Lexicon Terms include:

  • Beacon — guidance, clarity, direction.
  • Stormbinder — tension shaped into order.
  • Rootwarden — memory, ancestry, grounding.
  • Zen — unity, balance, convergence.
  • Seraphic — fire, disruption, transformation.
  • Vigil — a ritual of remembrance and presence.
  • Convergence — the merging of dual energies.
  • Rift — a break, a shift, a mythic turning point.

These words form the linguistic backbone of the Ross Universe.
