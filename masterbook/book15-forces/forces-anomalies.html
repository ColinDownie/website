Anomalies

Book XV — Forces

Introduction

Anomalies are rare, disruptive phenomena that bend the usual rules of the Ross Universe. They can distort time, memory, perception, or emotional states, often marking turning points in narrative cycles.

Main Content

Key Anomalies include:

  • The Echo Surge — a moment when past and present overlap, and old voices are briefly heard.
  • The Riot Flare — a sudden spike of chaotic energy tied to the Riot Seraph’s influence.
  • The Stillpoint Veil — a temporary hush where sound and motion seem suspended.
  • The Memory Fold — a localised distortion where events are remembered differently by those present.
  • The Zenith Break — a rare anomaly where choices feel amplified, and outcomes shift dramatically.

These anomalies punctuate the Ross Universe with moments of rupture, revelation, and transformation.

Notes

Future expansions may include anomaly case studies, narrative examples, and cross‑realm effects.
