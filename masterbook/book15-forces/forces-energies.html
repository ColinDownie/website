Energies

Book XV — Forces

Introduction

Energies are emotional and symbolic currents that move through people, places, and stories. They are not visible, but their effects are felt in behaviour, atmosphere, and narrative direction.

Main Content

Key Energies include:

  • Calmfield — a stabilising energy associated with Green, reflection, and steady focus.
  • Fireline — a charged current linked to Orange, urgency, and creative risk.
  • Resonance Flow — the subtle energy of music, memory, and emotional alignment.
  • Succession Weight — the felt pressure of responsibility, legacy, and future expectation.
  • Zen Pulse — a rare state of balanced energy when dual forces move in harmony.

These energies shape the emotional climate of scenes, rituals, and character arcs.

Notes

Future expansions may include energy diagrams, cross‑book references, and ritual modulation techniques.
