Forces Overview

Book XV — Forces

Introduction

Forces are the invisible currents that shape the Ross Universe. They include elemental patterns, emotional energies, and rare anomalies that bend the rules of the world. These forces influence landscapes, structures, creatures, and the inner lives of the clans.

Main Content

This book introduces three major categories of forces:

  • Elements — foundational environmental forces such as wind, water, stone, and flame.
  • Energies — emotional and symbolic currents that move through people and places.
  • Anomalies — rare, disruptive phenomena that alter perception, memory, or fate.

Together, these forces form the unseen architecture of the Ross Universe — the currents beneath every story.

Notes

Future expansions may include force diagrams, seasonal cycles, and cross‑realm influence maps.
