Elements

Book XV — Forces

Introduction

Elements are the foundational environmental forces of the Ross Universe. They shape weather, terrain, and atmosphere, and often mirror emotional states and narrative beats.

Main Content

Key Elements include:

  • Highland Wind — a clear, cutting breeze associated with vigilance and awakening.
  • Riverflow — the steady movement of water tied to memory, continuity, and emotional release.
  • Stormcurrent — turbulent weather linked to conflict, tension, and imminent change.
  • Rootstone — the deep, unmoving strength of earth, tied to ancestry and grounding.
  • Skyfire — rare, vivid light phenomena associated with revelation and turning points.

These elements provide the environmental rhythm of the Ross Universe — the backdrop to every scene.

Notes

Future expansions may include elemental cycles, realm‑specific variations, and ritual uses.
