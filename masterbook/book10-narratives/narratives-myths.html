Myths

Book X — Narratives

Introduction

Myths are the foundational stories of the Ross Universe. They explain origins, define values, and preserve the symbolic truths of the dynasty. Myths are not literal histories — they are emotional and cultural anchors that shape identity across generations.

Main Content

Key Myths include:

  • The Three Forebears — the mythic tale of Ealdric the Beacon, Maerwyn the Stormbinder, and Tharion the Rootwarden.
  • The Birth of the Dual Clans — the origin story of Green and Orange, their divergence, and their destined reconciliation.
  • The Starmaker’s Descent — the legend of the cosmic muse who brought music, rhythm, and emotional resonance to the House.
  • The Zen Vessel — the myth explaining how unity emerges from duality, and how harmony is achieved through balance.
  • The Riot Seraph — the tale of the disruptive force that brings necessary chaos and transformation to stagnant eras.

These myths form the cultural backbone of the dynasty — shaping rituals, influencing realms, and guiding the emotional evolution of its people.

Notes

Future expansions may include illustrated myths, oral tradition scripts, and generational reinterpretations.
