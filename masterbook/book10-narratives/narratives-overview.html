Narratives Overview

Book X — Narratives

Introduction

Narratives are the living stories of the Ross Universe. They weave together lineage, realms, rituals, and mythic entities into emotional journeys that define the dynasty. These stories are not fixed — they evolve with each generation.

Main Content

This chapter introduces the three major narrative structures:

  • Arcs — character and emotional journeys that unfold over time.
  • Myths — foundational stories that shape identity, culture, and belief.
  • Cycles — repeating patterns of growth, conflict, and renewal within the dynasty.

Together, these narrative forms create the storytelling engine of the Ross Universe — a living mythology that grows with the House and its people.

Notes

Future expansions may include narrative timelines, character journeys, and mythic story sequences.
