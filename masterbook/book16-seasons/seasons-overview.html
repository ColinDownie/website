Seasons Overview

Book XVI — Seasons

Introduction

Seasons define the temporal and emotional cycles of the Ross Universe. They shape atmosphere, behaviour, rituals, and narrative rhythm. Each season carries its own symbolism, weather patterns, and emotional resonance.

Main Content

This book introduces three major seasonal layers:

  • Natural Seasons — environmental cycles of weather, growth, and change.
  • Emotional Seasons — inner cycles experienced by clans and characters.
  • Mythic Seasons — rare cosmic cycles tied to lineage, prophecy, and transformation.

Together, these seasons create the temporal architecture of the Ross Universe — the pulse beneath every story.

Notes

Future expansions may include seasonal calendars, ritual alignments, and cross‑realm variations.
