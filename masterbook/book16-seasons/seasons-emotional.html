Emotional Seasons

Book XVI — Seasons

Introduction

Emotional Seasons describe the inner cycles experienced by clans, characters, and lineages. These seasons influence behaviour, decision‑making, and narrative arcs.

Main Content

Key Emotional Seasons include:

  • Calm Bloom — a period of clarity, balance, and steady emotional grounding.
  • Firecrest — a charged season of creativity, urgency, and expressive risk.
  • Echofall — a reflective season tied to memory, introspection, and ancestral resonance.
  • Stormwake — a turbulent season of change, conflict, and emotional upheaval.

These emotional seasons shape the inner lives of the Ross Universe’s characters.

Notes

Future expansions may include emotional cycle charts, clan‑specific patterns, and narrative triggers.
