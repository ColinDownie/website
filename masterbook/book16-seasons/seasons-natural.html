Natural Seasons

Book XVI — Seasons

Introduction

Natural Seasons are the environmental cycles that shape landscapes, creatures, and daily life. Each season carries distinct weather, colours, and emotional undertones.

Main Content

Key Natural Seasons include:

  • Dawnrise — a season of renewal, soft light, and quiet beginnings.
  • Highsun — a bright, energetic season tied to growth, clarity, and outward action.
  • Fallshade — a cooling season of reflection, harvest, and emotional grounding.
  • Deepwinter — a stark, still season associated with endurance, memory, and inner strength.

These natural seasons set the environmental rhythm of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include weather charts, seasonal migrations, and realm‑specific variations.
