Mythic Seasons

Book XVI — Seasons

Introduction

Mythic Seasons are rare cosmic cycles tied to prophecy, lineage, and transformation. These seasons reshape fate, alter energies, and mark turning points in the dynasty’s story.

Main Content

Key Mythic Seasons include:

  • The Beacon Cycle — a season of guidance, revelation, and ancestral clarity.
  • The Stormbinder Phase — a turbulent cycle where chaos can be shaped or redirected.
  • The Rootwarden Slumber — a deep, grounding season tied to memory and protection.
  • The Zen Convergence — a rare alignment where dual energies merge into harmony.
  • The Seraphic Rift — a volatile season of disruption, transformation, and creative rebirth.

These mythic seasons mark the great turning points of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include cosmic calendars, convergence charts, and prophecy references.
