Clan Soundscapes

Book XIX — Music

Introduction

Each clan and energy type carries its own sonic identity. These soundscapes shape rituals, emotional states, and the atmosphere of scenes within the Ross Universe.

Main Content

Core Soundscapes include:

  • Green — soft percussion, warm bass, steady rhythms, reflective chords.
  • Orange — sharp drums, bright guitars, urgent tempos, fire‑edge synths.
  • Zen — dual‑layered harmonies, balanced pulses, blended tonalities.
  • Seraphic — distorted choirs, glitch‑fire textures, volatile crescendos.

These soundscapes define the emotional grammar of the clans and energies.

Notes

Future expansions may include ritual playlists, sound‑to‑colour mappings, and emotional modulation charts.
