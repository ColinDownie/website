Music Overview

Book XIX — Music

Introduction

Music is the emotional architecture of the Ross Universe. It shapes rituals, avatars, mythic cycles, and the inner lives of clans and characters. Every track, rhythm, and chord progression carries lineage, memory, and transformation.

Main Content

This book introduces three major musical layers:

  • The Starmaker Layer — the musical DNA of the dynasty.
  • Clan Soundscapes — the sonic identities of Green, Orange, Zen, and Seraphic forces.
  • Mythic Tracks — the cosmic songs that shape fate and emotional law.

Together, these layers form the sonic soul of the Ross Universe.
