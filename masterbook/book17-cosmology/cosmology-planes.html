Planes

Book XVII — Cosmology

Introduction

Planes are the major layers of existence that lie beyond the physical world. Each plane has its own rules, energies, and mythic inhabitants.

Main Content

Key Planes include:

  • The Luminous Plane — realm of guidance, visions, and ancestral echoes.
  • The Tempest Veil — a shifting plane of storms, conflict, and transformation.
  • The Rootdeep — a subterranean plane of memory, protection, and ancient truth.
  • The Dual Horizon — a liminal plane where Green and Orange energies meet.
  • The Seraphic Rift — a volatile plane of fire, disruption, and rebirth.

These planes form the multi‑layered structure of the Ross Universe’s cosmology.

Notes

Future expansions may include planar maps, travel rules, and cross‑plane interactions.
