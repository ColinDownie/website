Origins

Book XVII — Cosmology

Introduction

Origins describe the first moments of the Ross Universe — the emergence of light, lineage, and the Forebears. These myths explain how realms formed, how forces awakened, and how destiny began.

Main Content

Key Origin Myths include:

  • The First Beacon — the moment Ealdric’s light split the void and shaped direction.
  • The Stormbinding — Maerwyn’s taming of chaos, forming the first winds and waves.
  • The Rootward Rise — Tharion’s grounding force creating land, memory, and ancestry.
  • The Dual Spark — the birth of Green and Orange energies, destined to divide and reunite.
  • The Seraphic Fracture — the cosmic disruption that introduced transformation and fire.

These origins form the mythic foundation of the Ross Universe’s cosmology.

Notes

Future expansions may include creation diagrams, lineage timelines, and mythic commentary.
