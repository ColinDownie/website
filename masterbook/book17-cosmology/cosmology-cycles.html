Cosmic Cycles

Book XVII — Cosmology

Introduction

Cosmic Cycles are the repeating patterns that govern fate, seasons, energies, and lineage. These cycles influence prophecy, timing, and the emotional tides of the clans.

Main Content

Key Cosmic Cycles include:

  • The Beacon Orbit — a cycle of clarity, revelation, and directional change.
  • The Stormbinder Spiral — a repeating pattern of conflict, tension, and renewal.
  • The Rootwarden Loop — a grounding cycle tied to ancestry, memory, and protection.
  • The Dual Convergence — a rare alignment where Green and Orange energies reconcile.
  • The Seraphic Pulse — a volatile cycle of disruption, creativity, and rebirth.

These cycles shape the long‑term rhythm of the Ross Universe.
