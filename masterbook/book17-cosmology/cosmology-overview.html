Cosmology Overview

Book XVII — Cosmology

Introduction

Cosmology defines the structure, origins, and governing principles of the Ross Universe. It explains how realms connect, how forces move, and how mythic cycles shape fate. This book reveals the deep architecture beneath all stories.

Main Content

Cosmology is divided into three major layers:

  • Origins — the creation myths, first forces, and ancestral beginnings.
  • Planes — the major layers of existence beyond the physical world.
  • Cycles — the repeating cosmic patterns that influence realms and lineages.

Together, these layers form the cosmic blueprint of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include star maps, planar diagrams, and mythic timelines.
