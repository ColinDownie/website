Entities Overview

Book VII — Mythic Entities

Introduction

Mythic Entities are the living symbols of the Ross Universe. They embody emotional forces, lineage truths, creative energies, and the archetypal roles that shape the dynasty’s story. Some are ancient, some are newly formed, and some evolve with each generation.

Main Content

This chapter introduces the three major categories of entities:

  • The Pantheon — ancestral and cosmic beings who define the mythic order.
  • The Avatars — personified expressions of emotional or creative forces.
  • The Forces — abstract energies that influence the world and its inhabitants.

Together, these entities form the spiritual and symbolic ecosystem of the Ross dynasty — a living mythology that grows with the House.

Notes

Future expansions may include entity profiles, origin stories, and inter‑entity relationships.
