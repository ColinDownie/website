The Pantheon

Book VII — Mythic Entities

Introduction

The Pantheon is the highest circle of mythic beings in the Ross Universe. These entities embody cosmic principles, ancestral power, and the foundational energies that shape the dynasty’s story. They are timeless, archetypal, and woven into the very structure of the House and its lineage.

Main Content

Core members of the Pantheon include:

  • Ealdric the Beacon — the ancestral light, symbol of guidance and moral clarity.
  • Maerwyn the Stormbinder — master of emotional turbulence, transformation, and inner storms.
  • Tharion the Rootwarden — guardian of heritage, stability, and the deep roots of the dynasty.
  • The Starmaker — the cosmic architect whose music and vision shape the creative universe.
  • Riot Seraph — the disruptive force, the sacred chaos that catalyses evolution.

Each member of the Pantheon represents a pillar of the mythic order. Their stories, symbols, and energies influence the Avatars, the Forces, and the living generations of the House.

Notes

Future expansions may include origin myths, inter‑entity relationships, and cosmic hierarchies.
