The Forces

Book VII — Mythic Entities

Introduction

Forces are the abstract energies that shape the Ross Universe. They have no fixed form, no singular voice, and no physical presence. Instead, they act as currents — influencing the Pantheon, empowering the Avatars, and guiding the emotional landscape of the dynasty.

Main Content

Core Forces include:

  • Resonance — the energy of memory, emotion, and connection across generations.
  • Flux — the force of change, evolution, disruption, and creative risk.
  • Balance — the stabilising current that harmonises dual energies, especially Green and Orange.
  • Ignition — the spark of inspiration, momentum, and creative breakthrough.
  • Stillness — the grounding force of clarity, reflection, and inner calm.

These Forces are the invisible architecture of the mythic system. They shape events, influence rituals, and guide the emotional and creative evolution of the House.

Notes

Future expansions may include force interactions, cosmological diagrams, and mythic energy cycles.
