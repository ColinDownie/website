Thresholds

Book VIII — Realms

Introduction

Thresholds are the liminal spaces of the Ross Universe — the transitional zones where energies shift, identities evolve, and realms intersect. They are neither one place nor another, but the moment of becoming between them.

Main Content

Key Thresholds include:

  • The Crossing Path — the ritual walkway used during clan transitions and generational rites.
  • The Veilgate — the shimmering boundary between the physical world and the Ancestral Veil.
  • The Echo Bridge — a liminal span where memories surface and emotional truths reveal themselves.
  • The Flux Rift — a volatile threshold where creative chaos and transformation collide.
  • The Harmonised Door — the point where Green and Orange energies resolve into unity, opening the Zen state.

Thresholds are moments of choice, change, and revelation. They mark the turning points in the dynasty’s story and the emotional evolution of its characters.

Notes

Future expansions may include threshold rituals, crossing ceremonies, and inter‑realm travel sequences.
