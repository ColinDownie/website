Realms Overview

Book VIII — Realms

Introduction

Realms are the mythic, symbolic, and narrative spaces of the Ross Universe. They represent emotional landscapes, ancestral territories, creative dimensions, and the thresholds between worlds. Each realm carries its own logic, atmosphere, and purpose.

Main Content

This chapter introduces the three major categories of realms:

  • Territories — grounded spaces tied to lineage, clans, and lived experience.
  • Dimensions — abstract or metaphysical spaces shaped by emotion, memory, or myth.
  • Thresholds — transitional zones where energies shift, merge, or transform.

Together, these realms form the spatial architecture of the dynasty — a world that mirrors its emotional and mythic depth.

Notes

Future expansions may include maps, diagrams, realm histories, and inter‑realm pathways.
