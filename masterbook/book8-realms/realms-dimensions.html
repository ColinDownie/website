Dimensions

Book VIII — Realms

Introduction

Dimensions are the metaphysical spaces of the Ross Universe — emotional, symbolic, or mythic environments that exist beyond physical territory. They are shaped by memory, lineage, creativity, and the forces that flow through the dynasty.

Main Content

Key Dimensions include:

  • The Memory Field — a dimension woven from echoes, stories, and emotional resonance.
  • The Creative Ether — the space where ideas form, dissolve, and reform before entering the physical world.
  • The Ancestral Veil — the liminal dimension where past generations observe, guide, and influence.
  • The Dual Current — the energetic dimension where Green and Orange forces flow, clash, and harmonise.
  • The Stillpoint — a silent, infinite dimension of clarity, reflection, and inner equilibrium.

Dimensions are not travelled in the physical sense — they are entered through emotion, ritual, memory, or creative state. They form the invisible architecture of the Ross Universe.

Notes

Future expansions may include dimensional diagrams, access rituals, and inter‑dimensional pathways.
