Territories

Book VIII — Realms

Introduction

Territories are the grounded, lineage‑anchored spaces of the Ross Universe. These are places tied to clans, heritage, lived experience, and the emotional geography of the dynasty. They form the physical layer of the mythic world.

Main Content

Key Territories include:

  • The Clanlands — the divided yet intertwined regions of Green and Orange, each with its own culture and emotional tone.
  • Ross House Grounds — the ancestral estate, seat of lineage, and centre of the dynasty’s mythic gravity.
  • The Studio Quarter — the creative district where ideas, art, and music take physical form.
  • The Archive Vault — a subterranean territory where artefacts, documents, and memory are preserved.
  • The Pilgrim Roads — pathways connecting territories, used for rites, transitions, and generational journeys.

Territories provide structure, history, and physical grounding. They are the places where myth meets reality, and where the dynasty’s story becomes lived experience.

Notes

Future expansions may include maps, territorial histories, and clan‑specific regions.
