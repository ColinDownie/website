Lineage Mythos

Book V — Lineage

Introduction

The Lineage Mythos is the narrative heart of the Ross dynasty. It weaves together ancestral stories, symbolic events, and emotional truths into a unified mythic continuum. This mythos is not fixed — it evolves with each generation.

Main Content

Core elements of the Lineage Mythos include:

  • The Origin Flame — the spark that ignited the dynasty’s creative and emotional legacy.
  • The Three Pillars — Beacon, Stormbinder, Rootwarden — whose virtues shaped the House’s foundations.
  • The Great Convergence — the moment when lineage, creativity, and emotional balance aligned to form the modern House.
  • The Eternal Thread — the symbolic line connecting ancestors, present creators, and future heirs.

The mythos serves as both history and prophecy. It explains where the dynasty came from, and hints at where it is destined to go.

Notes

Future expansions may include mythic cycles, symbolic artefacts, and lineage parables.
