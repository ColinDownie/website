Ancestors

Book V — Lineage

Introduction

The ancestors of the Ross dynasty form the mythic foundation of the House. Their stories, virtues, and emotional signatures echo through every generation that follows. They are the first keepers of lineage.

Main Content

The ancestral pantheon includes figures whose lives shaped the values and emotional architecture of the House. Each ancestor represents a pillar of the dynasty:

  • The Beacon — symbol of guidance, clarity, and moral light.
  • The Stormbinder — master of emotional turbulence and transformation.
  • The Rootwarden — guardian of heritage, stability, and continuity.

These archetypal ancestors influence the present generation, offering strength, wisdom, and emotional resonance. Their presence is felt in rituals, symbols, and the very structure of the House itself.

Notes

Future expansions may include detailed ancestral biographies, artefacts, and lineage timelines.
