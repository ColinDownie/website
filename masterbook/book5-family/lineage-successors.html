Successors

Book V — Lineage

Introduction

Successors carry the dynasty forward. They inherit not only the name, but the emotional truth, creative spirit, and mythic responsibilities of the House. A successor is both a continuation and an evolution.

Main Content

The Ross Lineage recognises successors on multiple levels:

  • The Heir of Heart — the one who carries the emotional core of the dynasty.
  • The Heir of Craft — the one who inherits the creative engine of the House.
  • The Heir of Story — the one who continues the mythos, expanding the narrative.
  • The Heir of Legacy — the one who protects the House’s values and lineage.

A single successor may embody all roles, or they may be shared across generations and individuals. What matters is continuity — the eternal thread that binds past to future.

Notes

Future expansions may include successor rites, generational transitions, and symbolic inheritance rituals.
