Lineage Overview

Book V — Lineage

Introduction

Lineage is the heart of the Ross dynasty. It is the thread that connects ancestors, present creators, and future successors into a single, unbroken story. Every act within the House is rooted in this continuum.

Main Content

This chapter introduces the structure of lineage within the mythos: the ancestral origins, the living generation, and the future heirs. Lineage is not only biological — it is emotional, creative, and symbolic.

The Ross Lineage is defined by: - heritage - emotional truth - creative evolution - mythic continuity

Notes

Future expansions may include family trees, ancestral stories, and successor rites.
