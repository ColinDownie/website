Legacy Symbols

Book VI — Legacy

Introduction

Symbols are the visual heartbeat of the Ross dynasty. They condense lineage, emotion, and myth into forms that can be carried, worn, engraved, or ritualised. A symbol is more than an image — it is a memory made visible.

Main Content

Core symbols within the Legacy vault include:

  • The Crest — the primary emblem of the House, representing unity and lineage.
  • The Eternal Thread — a line motif symbolising continuity across generations.
  • The Origin Flame — the spark of creativity and emotional truth.
  • The Dual Sigils — Green and Orange marks representing balance and duality.
  • The Zen Circle — the symbol of harmony, integration, and resolved dual forces.

Each symbol carries a story, a purpose, and a place within the rituals and architecture of the House. Together, they form the visual language of the dynasty.

Notes

Future expansions may include symbol variations, ceremonial applications, and design evolution.
