Legacy Archives

Book VI — Legacy

Introduction

The Legacy Archives preserve the history, artefacts, documents, and emotional milestones of the Ross dynasty. This is the vault where memory becomes record, and record becomes canon.

Main Content

The Archives contain:

  • The Masterbook Records — previous editions, sealed chapters, and lineage updates.
  • The Ancestral Documents — letters, decrees, and writings from earlier generations.
  • The Creative Vault — sketches, drafts, prototypes, and early works from the Studio.
  • The Clan Logs — records of Green and Orange cycles, rituals, and resolutions.
  • The Memory Repository — preserved emotional artefacts marking key transitions in the dynasty.

The Archives ensure that nothing meaningful is lost. Every era, every creation, every emotional truth is preserved for future generations to study, honour, and build upon.

Notes

Future expansions may include digital archives, sealed vaults, and generational access protocols.
