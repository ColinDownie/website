Legacy Items

Book VI — Legacy

Introduction

Legacy items are the physical artefacts of the Ross dynasty — objects that carry emotional weight, creative history, and symbolic meaning. These items act as anchors across generations.

Main Content

Key legacy items include:

  • The Lineage Ledger — the book that records major events, transitions, and ancestral truths.
  • The Studio Tools — brushes, pens, instruments, and devices used in the creation of iconic works.
  • The Clan Vessels — Green and Orange containers used in the Zen Pour ritual.
  • The Crest Seal — the stamp used to authenticate documents, artefacts, and House decrees.
  • The Memory Stones — symbolic objects representing key emotional milestones in the dynasty.

Each item carries a story — a moment in time, a creative breakthrough, or a lineage truth preserved in physical form.

Notes

Future expansions may include item catalogues, origin stories, and ceremonial usage guides.
