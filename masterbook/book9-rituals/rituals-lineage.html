Lineage Rituals

Book IX — Rituals

Introduction

Lineage Rituals honour the ancestral line of the Ross dynasty. These ceremonies preserve memory, recognise succession, and strengthen the bond between past, present, and future generations. They are the emotional spine of the House.

Main Content

Key Lineage Rituals include:

  • The Ancestral Calling — a quiet rite where names of forebears are spoken to invite guidance.
  • The Successor’s Step — the ceremonial moment when the next generation accepts their role.
  • The Keeper’s Vigil — a reflective ritual performed by protectors of the lineage.
  • The Memory Lanterns — lights released to honour those who have passed into the Veil.
  • The Bloodline Oath — a vow of stewardship, integrity, and continuity within the House.

These rituals ensure that the dynasty remains connected across time — each generation supported by the last, and preparing the way for the next.

Notes

Future expansions may include lineage trees, ceremonial objects, and generational rites of passage.
