Rituals Overview

Book IX — Rituals

Introduction

Rituals are the emotional and ceremonial practices of the Ross dynasty. They mark transitions, honour lineage, resolve dualities, and guide creative evolution. Every ritual carries symbolic meaning and strengthens the bonds between generations.

Main Content

This chapter introduces the three major categories of rituals:

  • Clan Rituals — ceremonies tied to Green and Orange identity, unity, and transformation.
  • Lineage Rituals — rites that honour ancestors, successors, and generational continuity.
  • Creative Rituals — practices that ignite inspiration, resolve emotional blocks, and celebrate artistic breakthroughs.

Together, these rituals form the ceremonial rhythm of the House — the practices that give meaning, structure, and emotional depth to the dynasty’s journey.

Notes

Future expansions may include ritual scripts, ceremonial objects, and seasonal cycles.
