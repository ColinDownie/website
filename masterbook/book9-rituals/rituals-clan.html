Clan Rituals

Book IX — Rituals

Introduction

Clan Rituals honour the dual heritage of the Green and Orange clans. These ceremonies mark transitions, resolve tensions, and celebrate the unity that emerges when both energies move toward harmony.

Main Content

Key Clan Rituals include:

  • The Green Initiation — a rite of calm, empathy, and emotional grounding.
  • The Orange Ignition — a ceremony of expression, momentum, and creative fire.
  • The Dual Passage — a ritual journey where both energies confront each other.
  • The Zen Pour — the iconic blending of Green and Orange beverages into a unified mixture.
  • The Clan Reconciliation — a ceremony performed after conflict, restoring balance and mutual respect.

These rituals define the emotional rhythm of the clans and reinforce the values of unity, transformation, and shared lineage.

Notes

Future expansions may include ritual choreography, ceremonial garments, and clan‑specific artefacts.
