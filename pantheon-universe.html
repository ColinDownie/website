Overview

The Pantheon Universe is the highest mythic layer of the Ross Dynasty — the realm of archetypes, divine forces, and cosmic entities who shape emotion, lineage, and transformation across all universes.

Here, the Starmaker, Riot Seraph, Archivist, Beacon, Stormbinder, and Rootwarden exist in their purest forms. The Pantheon is the source code of the mythos.
